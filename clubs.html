Explore Our School Clubs

Chess Club

A haven for chess enthusiasts and strategy game lovers. The club hosts tournaments, puzzle-solving sessions, and workshops to refine strategic thinking skills, making chess fun and competitive for all levels.

Art Club

This club encourages students to express their creativity through painting, drawing, photography, and digital art. Their events include art exhibitions, live painting sessions, and collaborative art projects.

Science Club

The TechnoSphere Club gathers students with a passion for science, technology, engineering, and innovation. They organize fairs, hackathons, and experiments to inspire creativity and practical problem-solving among members.

Music Club

This club celebrates the power of music by connecting performers and fans. From live performances to karaoke nights, they create a lively space for students to showcase their musical talents.

Programming Club

Focusing on programming and software development, this club empowers students to learn practical coding skills through bootcamps, coding challenges, and peer-led projects.

Movie Club
